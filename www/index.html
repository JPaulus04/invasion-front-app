How to Play
Commander's field manual

The Goal

Enemies spawn at the top and march down toward your Base at the bottom. Each breach costs HP — zero HP ends the run. Stop every wave using troops, lane infrastructure, and your Orbital Strike.

The Three Lanes

◀ Left — Control: EW Specialists + Sensor Arrays. Slows enemies.

◆ Center — Sustain: Medics + Med Stations. Heals your force & base.

▶ Right — Strike: Grenadiers + Snipers. Maximum kill output.

Controls

Lane buttons (▲ ◆ ▼) — switch active lane
⊕ Enlist — open troop picker, tap to deploy
▶ Wave — start next wave
☄ Orbital — launch strike (has cooldown)
⚗ Research — open lane & global upgrades
★ Career — view prestige & run history

Doctrines

⚡ Blitzkrieg — High dmg, fragile base.
🛡 Fortress — Durable, tight economy.
⚙ Logistics — Cheap troops, income scaling.
📡 EW Superiority — Slow & strip shields.
🎯 Artillery — Turrets + Orbital focus.

Prestige

Reach Wave 10 to Prestige. Resets the run and grants permanent Command Rank, income bonuses, and new unlocks.
